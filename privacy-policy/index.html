Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Game Font Tools is designed to work without requiring an account. Text you type into the generator is processed in your browser for normal tool usage and is not intentionally stored by us.

We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate usage, such as page views, device type, general geography, and traffic sources. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies according to Google's own policies.

Information you provide

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and message content so we can respond. We do not sell personal information.

Some pages link to official Roblox documentation and other external resources. Their privacy practices are controlled by those sites.

Contact
